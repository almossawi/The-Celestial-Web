A brief overview of the visualization

The visualization compares the majority market share of Web browsers in different countries across time.


Travel through time using your keyboard's left and right keys

Zooming the page in and out won't degrade quality

Red is the subset of countries that we may look at more closely*

This work is being developed by Ali Almossawi and the Metrics Team at Mozilla in San Francisco. For all inquiries, or if you're interested in collaborating, get in touch!  hello@mozillacosmos.com

Market share data from July 2008 to present is from statcounter.com. The background music is an excerpt from the album Heaven and Hell by Vangelis. The website is released under a Creative Commons license (CC BY-SA 3.0).

* Argentina, Austria, Australia, Brazil, Canada, Switzerland, China, Germany, Spain, France, UK, Indonesia,
  India, Italy, Japan, Mexico, Malaysia, Netherlands, Philippines, Portugal, Russia, Sweden, Turkey, United States, Vietnam
